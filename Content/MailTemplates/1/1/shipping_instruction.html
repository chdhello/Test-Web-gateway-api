Shipping Instructions
 
Packaging Material required
 
 
Scissors Bubble Wrap Tape P. Chips Corner Protectors New Box
 
Steps to Follow
Outer Packaging: Use the original box or any other box with sufficient crush space.
Wrapping Items: Each item should be wrapped individually in 2” layer of bubble wrap. The screen and buttons should be provided extra protection by taping or putting bubble wraps. Place the accessories on top of the device.
Internal Preparation: Place corner protectors in the box and safely place the device. Fill extra spaces with peanut chips.
Sealing: Seal the box both horizontally and vertically with packaging tape. Go over seams twice for extra hold. Place your shipping label on top of the box and cover it with clear packing tape to prevent tearing during shipping.
 
Do’s and Don’t
 
Use a box that's strong enough and correct size for the contents, ideally a new unused box
Remove any old barcoded labels, address labels or markings from the box
Wrap each item individually before placing them inside the box
Use as much cushioning material as possible
Device should be disassembled where possible
 
Re-use an old box where the walls of the box are weakened or crease
Let any item inside the box touch the inside walls of the box
Allow any movement of your items inside the
box
Using paper as a filler as the material
Include any CDs, DVDs, thumb drives, hard drives, SD cards, headphones or any other accessory
